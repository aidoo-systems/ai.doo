Why self-hosted

Your data stays where it belongs.

Self-hosted AI isn't just a deployment detail — it's a fundamentally different relationship between your organisation and its data.

01

Data never leaves

No API calls to external AI providers. Documents, queries, and results stay within your network perimeter. Auditable by design.

Private by default No egress
02

Runs on your hardware

A VPS, workstation, or dedicated GPU server. No cloud instances, no external runtime dependencies. You own the infrastructure.

VPS On-premise GPU optional
03

Air-gap capable

Works fully offline after initial setup. No internet dependency for inference — critical for classified, industrial, or disconnected environments.

Offline No cloud dependency
04

Predictable cost

No per-query, per-token, or seat-tier pricing surprises. One deployment, fixed infrastructure cost, unlimited internal usage.

Fixed cost Unlimited use
05

Compliance-ready

No third-party data processor under GDPR Article 28 — because there is none. Simplifies DPIAs, ISO 27001 scoping, and regulatory sign-off.

GDPR ISO 27001
06

Full auditability

Every query, model version, and access event logged locally. You own the audit trail — no vendor dashboard required.

Audit log Local storage
