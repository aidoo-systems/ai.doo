Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 February 2026

1. Introduction

This privacy policy explains how ai.doo ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our website at aidoo.biz and our products (including PIKA). We are trading as ai.doo and are subject to the Isle of Man Data Protection Act 2018, which mirrors the UK GDPR.

We have designed our website and products with privacy in mind. We do not run analytics platforms, advertising trackers, or sign-up forms that collect personal data.

2. Information we collect

Our web server automatically records standard access logs when you visit aidoo.biz. These logs may include:

  • Your IP address
  • Browser user agent string (browser type and version)
  • Pages requested and timestamps
  • HTTP status codes and response sizes

We do not operate any analytics platform (such as Google Analytics), display advertising, or collect data through forms on this website.

3. Google Fonts

This website loads fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com). When your browser requests a font file, Google receives your IP address and browser user agent as part of that request. This is the only meaningful third-party data touch point on this site.

For details on how Google handles this data, see Google Fonts privacy FAQ.

4. Our apps (PIKA)

PIKA is a self-hosted document intelligence application. It is designed to run entirely within your own infrastructure. Data you enter into PIKA — including documents and queries — is processed on your own systems. ai.doo does not receive, store, or have access to that data.

This policy will be updated as our products evolve and as specific hosting arrangements are confirmed.

5. Cookies

This website does not set any cookies. No tracking cookies, session cookies, or third-party cookies are used.

6. How we use information

Server access logs are used solely for security monitoring and troubleshooting purposes. We do not sell, share, or otherwise disclose log data to third parties, except where required by law.

7. Data retention

Server access logs are retained for approximately 90 days, after which they are automatically deleted.

8. Your rights

Under the Isle of Man Data Protection Act 2018 you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification of inaccurate data
  • Erasure of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Object to processing
  • Data portability
  • Lodge a complaint with the Isle of Man Information Commissioner (ico.org.im)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@aidoo.biz.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this policy or how we handle your data, please email hello@aidoo.biz.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page shows when it was last revised. Continued use of our website or products after any update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
