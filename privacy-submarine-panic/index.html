Privacy Policy — Submarine Panic

Last updated: June 2026

Submarine Panic is a mobile game built in Godot. It is designed to run entirely on your device, with no online features. This policy explains what the game stores and confirms that none of it leaves your phone. Our website: https://www.aidoo.biz.

Data we collect

None that leaves your device. Submarine Panic does not create an account, and it sends no data to us or to anyone else.

The game does keep a small save file on your device so it can remember your progress between sessions. That file stores:

  • Your settings, such as volume, haptics, and the CRT visual toggle
  • Your high score and current rank
  • Records of your past patrols (the in-game incident summaries)

This information stays in local storage on your device and is never transmitted.

Network and online features

The current version of Submarine Panic ships without the internet permission. It makes no network connections and works fully offline. There are no online accounts and no cloud saves.

We may add an optional online leaderboard in a future update. The next section describes how that would work. It is not part of the game today, and we will update this policy and the app's permissions before any such feature ships.

Optional online leaderboard (planned)

If we add a leaderboard, taking part would be optional, and you could play the whole game without ever submitting a score. Submitting a score would send a small amount of data to a developer-hosted leaderboard service:

  • A persistent anonymous player ID
  • An optional display name that you choose and can change or clear
  • Your score, run duration, and difficulty
  • A timestamp for the entry

This would not include your real name, contacts, or any other personal details. When the feature is released, we will confirm its exact behaviour here and explain how to request removal of your leaderboard entry.

Data we do not collect

We do not collect location, contacts, photos, microphone, camera, or payment data. There are no ads, no third-party analytics SDKs, and no tracking of any kind.

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data, because we never receive any in the first place. Your save file stays with you.

Storage and deletion

All game data is stored locally on your device. To remove it completely, uninstall the game or clear its data in your device settings. There is nothing for us to delete on our side.

Children's privacy

Submarine Panic is a general-audience game and is not directed at children under 13. The current game collects no personal information and has no online features, so there is nothing for a child to share with us. If the optional leaderboard above is added, it will not ask for personal details.

Changes to this policy

If the game gains a feature that changes how data is handled, such as an optional online leaderboard, we will update this policy before that feature ships.
